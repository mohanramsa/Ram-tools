What Is RamTools?

RamTools is a free online platform built for electrical engineers, students, HR professionals, and curious learners. Every tool on this website is 100% free, works directly in your browser, and requires no login or installation. From advanced circuit simulation to salary slip generation, RamTools brings practical engineering utilities together in one place.

Our Free Tools at a Glance

The Electrical Circuit Simulator lets you build and simulate circuits online — drag and drop resistors, capacitors, inductors, motors, and transformers onto a canvas, connect them with wires, and run the simulation to see live current flow and voltage readings. It works on both desktop and mobile without any download.

The AC & DC Circuit Analyzer calculates voltage, current, impedance, power factor, and phase angle for Series, Parallel, RC, RL, and RLC circuits instantly. Simply select your circuit type, switch between AC and DC mode, and adjust the sliders — all results update in real time with waveform graphs and phasor diagrams.

The Electrical Formulas reference covers Ohm's Law, power calculations, motor current calculations, cable size & bus bar size calculations, Battery size calculations.

The Solar Calculator helps you size solar panels, estimate energy output, calculate battery backup requirements, and evaluate savings for rooftop or off-grid solar systems — ideal for homeowners and solar installers alike.

The Relay Testing Guide provides step-by-step commissioning procedures for overcurrent relays, differential relays, earth fault relays, and protection relay systems — trusted by field engineers and electrical supervisors.

The SalaryPro Salary Slip Generator creates professional salary slips instantly. Enter employee details, CTC, HRA, PF, and other deductions — get a formatted payslip ready to print or share. Used by HR managers and small business owners.

The AI Formula Generator lets you describe your electrical engineering problem in plain language and get the right formula and solution generated by AI — perfect for students and engineers who want quick answers without flipping through textbooks. Also including Dyn11 vector group tests, hi-pot tests, magnetising current tests, and magnetic balance tests — all explained with practical steps and IEC/IEEE references.

For a fun and interactive learning experience, our Electrical Quiz tests your wiring and circuit knowledge through multiple-choice questions — a great way to prepare for exams or licensing tests. And for a quieter side of learning, the Spiritual Blog offers thoughtful articles on mindfulness, devotion, and life's deeper questions.

Frequently Asked Questions

Is RamTools completely free to use?

Yes. Every tool on RamTools is 100% free — no subscriptions, no sign-up, no hidden charges. All tools run directly in your browser on any device.

What electrical tools does RamTools offer?

RamTools offers a free electrical circuit simulator, AC & DC circuit analyzer, electrical formulas reference, relay testing guide, solar calculator, AI formula generator, and an electrical quiz game.

Can I use RamTools on my mobile phone?

Yes. All RamTools tools are fully mobile-responsive. The circuit simulator, salary generator, and all calculators work on Android and iOS browsers — no app download needed.

How does the free Circuit Simulator work?

Open the Circuit Simulator, drag components from the left panel onto the canvas, connect them with the Wire tool, click components to set values, then press RUN SIMULATION. On mobile, tap a component then tap the canvas to place it. Current flow animates live on the wires.

How does the AC & DC Circuit Analyzer work?

Open the AC & DC Circuit Analyzer, select a circuit type (Series, Parallel, RC, RL, or RLC), choose AC or DC mode, turn the circuit switch ON, and move the sliders. Voltage, current, impedance, power factor, phase angle, and waveforms all update in real time.

Yes. The SalaryPro tool lets you enter employee details, CTC, HRA, PF, and deductions, then instantly generates a professional salary slip — completely free to print or save.
