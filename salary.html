About Salary Calculator

This salary calculator helps employees, HR professionals, and small business owners calculate salary components such as basic pay, HRA, PF, and deductions easily. It simplifies payroll calculations and generates accurate salary slips instantly.

How to Use

  1. Enter employee salary details
  2. Add allowances and deductions
  3. Click calculate
  4. Download or view salary slip

Example

If basic salary is ₹20,000 and allowances are ₹5,000, total gross salary becomes ₹25,000. After deductions like PF and tax, net salary is calculated.

    📖 How To Use SalaryPro
    1
    Fill Company & Employee Info

    Enter company name, pay month/year, employee name, ID, designation, department, DOJ, bank account and PAN in the top section.

    2
    Enter Earnings

    Fill Basic Salary, HRA, Travel Allowance, Medical Allowance, Special Allowance and Other Allowance. All are monthly full-month values.

    3
    Enter Deductions

    Enter PF (use Auto-calculate for 12% of Basic), Professional Tax, TDS. Use preset chips to add Advance/EMI, Suspense Cash, Insurance, Canteen etc. Drag items to reorder.

    4
    Pro-Rata (Optional)

    If employee worked partial month, enter Total Days (e.g. 31) and Days Present (e.g. 25). Leave blank for full month. Formula: Gross × (25 ÷ 31) = ₹16,129

    5
    Choose Pro-Rata Method

    Option 1: All components (Basic+HRA+TA+Medical+Special) get adjusted — used by IT companies & startups.
    Option 2: Only Basic salary adjusted, HRA/TA/Medical stay at full amount — used by PSUs & large corporates.

    6
    Add Employee & Print

    Click "Add Employee" to save. Go to the Salary Slip tab for a print-ready slip. Click "Print / Save PDF" to save as PDF.
