Our Story

About RamTools

Built with love to make useful tools freely available for everyone.

Who We Are

RamTools is a free online utility platform built to empower individuals with practical tools, engineering knowledge, and spiritual inspiration — all in one place. We believe that helpful resources should be accessible to everyone, without paywalls.

Our Mission

Our mission is simple: create high-quality, free tools that save time, aid learning, and support personal growth. Whether you're an HR professional generating salary slips, an electrical engineer looking up formulas, a student learning through our quiz game, or someone seeking spiritual nourishment — RamTools has something for you.

What We Offer

SalaryPro — A professional salary slip generator that calculates gross pay, deductions (PF, PT, TDS), and net salary instantly. Generate and print polished salary slips in seconds.

Electrical Formulas — A comprehensive reference of electrical engineering formulas covering Ohm's Law, AC circuits, motors, transformers, and more. With built-in calculators.

AI Electrical Formula Generator — Describe your problem in plain English and get the right formula and explanation instantly.

Electrical Quiz — An interactive quiz to test and reinforce your electrical knowledge. Fun, educational, and rewarding.

Spiritual Blog — Thoughtful, well-researched articles on spirituality, mindfulness, Bhakti, Karma, the Gita, and more — written to inspire and guide.

Our Values

We are committed to keeping all our tools 100% free forever. We do not sell your data. We do not display intrusive ads. We simply build, share, and hope it helps someone's day go a little better.
