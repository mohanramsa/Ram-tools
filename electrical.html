    🔴 Transformer Short Circuit Current & Stability Check
    Enter nameplate and test data from the impedance (short circuit) test to calculate HV & LV short circuit currents.
    📋 Nameplate Data
    🔬 Short Circuit Test (Field / Lab Measurements)
    Average of 3 line voltages (RY+YB+BR)/3 measured during SC test
    Line current measured on HV side during SC test
    📐 Formula Reference (as per standard SC test procedure)
    Step Calculation Formula / Note
    1. HV Rated Current I_HV = kVA×1000 / (√3 × V_HV×1000) Full load current on HV winding
    2. LV Rated Current I_LV = kVA×1000 / (√3 × V_LV×1000) Full load current on LV winding
    3. Impedance Voltage (Vz) Vz = (%Z / 100) × V_HV × 1000 Full impedance voltage in volts — if this voltage applied on HV with LV shorted → rated current flows
    4. HV SC Current at Test Voltage Isc_HV = I_HV_rated × (V_test / Vz) Proportional: since V_test < Vz, Isc_HV < rated. Cross-check against measured value.
    5. LV SC Current (Normal Tap) Isc_LV = Isc_HV × (V_HV / V_LV) Short circuit current reflected to LV side via voltage ratio
    6. Stability Check Measured Isc vs Calculated Isc If measured ≈ calculated (within ±5%) → ✅ PASS. Deviation >5% → ⚠️ investigate
