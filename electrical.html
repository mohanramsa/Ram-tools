What is Electrical Formulas Calculator?

This electrical calculator helps engineers, electricians, and students quickly calculate important electrical values such as voltage, current, resistance, power, and cable size. It is useful for real-world applications like motor installation, panel design, and troubleshooting electrical systems.

How to Use This Tool

  1. Select the required calculator
  2. Enter known values
  3. Click calculate
  4. View instant result

Example

If voltage is 230V and current is 5A, power = 230 × 5 = 1150 Watts.

Who Can Use This Electrical Tool?

This tool is useful for electrical engineers, diploma students, maintenance technicians, and professionals involved in testing and commissioning of electrical systems.
